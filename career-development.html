Career Development

The Process

With the consumer desiring employment, their family members, and other support resources, the Career Development program involves the following.
Once the consumer is employed, the program provides the ongoing support to assure full on-site training with consumer, supervisors, and co-workers. Also assisting supervisors and co-workers in understand the consumer's abilities and how to deal with their unique issues. We provide liaison support to the consumer and employer and regularly interact with and advice family, and friends.

Our Process Can Help You:

- Assessing career objectives, interests, and experience.
- Designing a plan to build on strengths and capabilities.
- Executing a targeted job search.
- Assisting in interviewing and job placement.

The program is also designed to continually evaluate the consumers work situation relative to their career objectives. As they expand their experience and abilties and wish to move ahead in their careers, we start the process again. assisting them in finding new, more challenging employment that helps move them towards their goals.

The program focuses on assisting adults with disabilities in their efforts in to find and maintain employment.

Career Development has been a core service of the company since inception. We are proud to say that we have had the opportunity to help thousands of people with disabilities enjoy the rewards of meaningful, real-world, employment.
