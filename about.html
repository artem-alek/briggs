  • About Briggs & Associates

Jennifer Briggs

President, Briggs & Associates
"Our communities are not whole until all citizens are participating, valued, and contributing."

Briggs & Associates was founded in 1989 based on the philosophy that "anyone who has the desire has the ability to be successful in the workplace."


We have provided employment services to thousands of people including adults with developmental disabilities, adults with psychiatric disorders, and young people with disabilities transitioning from high school into work.


The company has no workshop or day-service center. All work is based in the community.
